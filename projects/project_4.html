BOOKLET

This group project consisted in interviewing an artist of our choice about his work ethic. With this in mind we chose ANDRÉ LETRIA, an editor and illustrator, based in Portugal. Inspired by his graphic style and some of his work, we decided to create the cover, jokingly portraying the artist's absence of hair. Through this idea, the illustration for the inside came easily.

Group: André Santos, Joana Cardoso, Matilde Domingues, Rita Cruz
